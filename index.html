The Author

Hello, I'm Tharun K, a self-published author who believes stories should stay with readers long after the final page. My writing explores love, loss, healing, and the emotions we often struggle to express. Every chapter I write carries a piece of my heart, hoping it finds a place in yours.

From Rebloomed Love to The Broken Red Cord, every book reflects a journey through emotions, relationships, and the quiet moments that shape us. Thank you for being here. This is only the beginning.
